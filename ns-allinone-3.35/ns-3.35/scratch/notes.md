1. What is DSRC?
    Dedicated Short-Range Communication is a wireless communication method designed for intelligent transportation systems. It allows vehicles to communicate directly with other vehicles, pedestrians, and roadside infrastructure in the licensed 5.9 GHz band without relying on cellular networks.

2. What is LLDP?
    LLDP means Link Layer Discovery Protocol. LLDP is a Layer 2 protocol used by network devices to discover their directly connected neighbors. LLDP is used by switches, routers, access points, and other network devices.
